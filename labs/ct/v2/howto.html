Thank you for downloading cottonTracks!

the app is organizing your history right now.

Meanwhile, let's take a look at how to use it.

Browse any subject on the web

cottonTracks automatically aggregates stories of your

favorite content when you browse the web.

In order to test it right away, visit a few web pages around

a subject you like (for example "Hayao Myazaki").

Click the c button

Our button is located on the top-right corner of your

browser. Click it and you will get access to everything

you have enjoyed in the past about the subject

you are currently browsing.

Browse your stories

You can see your latest and favorite stories from our

interface as well as all the content related to

what you are doing right now.

Explore and enjoy!

Play with the cards

Each video, map, article you have browsed are now

available in your story. You can read again your

favorite quotes, explore an interesting area or play again

your this footage that made you laugh so much.
